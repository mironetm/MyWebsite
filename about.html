About

I’m Mihaela Mironet, an artist and digital designer based in London, educated at Roehampton University. My work explores motion design, UX/UI, web design, illustration, and digital storytelling, with a focus on creating thoughtful and engaging visual experiences.

I’m passionate about visual storytelling and enjoy bringing ideas to life across digital, editorial, and interactive design. One of my key projects is She Travels Magazine, first released in May 2026, inspired by my love for solo travel and created as an empowering space for women through storytelling, editorial design, and illustration.

I’m especially drawn to editorial design, branding, and UX/UI, where I can combine visual storytelling with clear and engaging design.
