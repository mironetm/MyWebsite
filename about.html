About

I am Mihaela Mironet, an artist and digital designer based in London, educated at Roehampton University. My practice explores UX/UI, web design, and illustration.

I have worked on a range of projects across app design, websites, branding, and editorial design, which has helped me build a versatile approach to design across both digital and print media. Through these experiences, I have developed a strong understanding of how visual communication can shape the way people connect with ideas, stories, and digital experiences.

At the same time, I am passionate about visual storytelling and enjoy bringing ideas to life through digital, editorial, and interactive design. I like exploring how design can be both functional and expressive, creating work that is not only visually strong but also meaningful and accessible.
