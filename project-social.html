STUDY ABROAD, UNIVERSITY OF ROEHAMPTON

I completed a work placement with the Study Abroad Team at the University of Roehampton, where I contributed to their social media presence, mainly on Instagram. I filmed and edited short student interviews using CapCut, created and edited Instagram posts, and helped produce content explaining how to apply for study abroad opportunities. Additionally, I designed posters for International Week to support event promotion.

ART4SPACE, INTERVIEWS

I collaborated with Art4Space, a creative arts organisation based in Brixton that supports young people through accessible and community-focused art programmes. As part of this project, I filmed and edited interviews for an Facebook Reel and a YouTube video, highlighting their work and values. During the interviews, I asked team members about their Christmas workshop, how they support students in developing their creative skills, and what inspired them to join the Art4Space team. I was responsible for video editing and storytelling to ensure the content was engaging and suitable for social media platforms.
